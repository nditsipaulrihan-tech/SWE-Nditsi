Location & Directions

Heritage University is located at Biyem-Assi Yaounde, opposite Boulangerie Francaise

Map showing the location of Heritage University in Yaounde





Heritage University campus - Biyem-Assi, Yaounde. View on Google Maps

Getting Here

  • From Yaounde Centre
    1. Take a taxi or bus heading towards Carrefour Biyem-Assi.
    2. Ask to stop at "Boulangerie Francaise"
    3. Look Opposite the Boulangerie and you will see the campus
  • From Nsimalen Airport
    1. Take the airport shuttle to Yaounde Centre
    2. Follow directions from Yaounde Centre above
