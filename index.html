About Our Institution

Who We Are

Heritage University founded in 2017, is a leading institution in the field of engineering educattion, dedicated to providing high-quality accademic programs and research opportunities.

Our programs are accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education. and follows the HND (Higher National Diploma) curriculum framework. All progam are subject to annual review

Our Philosophy

At Heritage university, we believe in fostering innovation and critical thinking. We are commited to delivering an exceptional educational experience that prepares our students to excel in their choosen fields and contribute meaningfully to social. we say what we mean and we mean what we say
